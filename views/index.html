What is BrewBear?

BrewBear is a one-stop homebrew tracking suite. Store all of your beers & recipes and keep track of what you've currently got on tap!

My Bar

Add beers to your "bar". Customize your home taproom with kegs, bottles, and what's currently being served.

History & Trends

View your drinking history! See how often you drink, what your most popular drinking day of the week is, and what your most popular brews are plus much more!

What's On Tap?

Display next to your tap set up. Keep track of the estimated beer remaining in your kegs. Display what is currently being served in your homebrew bar!

I develop this application in my free time. Maintenance costs are pretty much zero at the moment, but the caffeine and beer that powers me through the development is not.

If you like what you see and would like to support me, buy me a coffee or a beer!

BrewBear is open source. What am I doing with the information you share by logging in? Nothing, it's just to make your experience a little more personable! What am I doing with your email address? Nothing, well, not true...I'll send updates about the site...unless you ask me to stop!

Don't believe me? Like I said, it's open source so head over to GitHub and look at the code if you want to see for yourself...or contribute, I'd love for code contributions as well
